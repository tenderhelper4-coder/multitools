Delay Penalty Calculator (Liquidated Damages)

This tool calculates Liquidated Damages (LD) for delay in project completion as per Government Tender norms.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is LD?
LD is penalty charged for delay in project completion.

2. How is LD calculated?
LD = Contract Value × LD Rate × Delay Duration (subject to max cap).

3. What is max cap?
Usually 5%–10% of contract value in Govt tenders.
